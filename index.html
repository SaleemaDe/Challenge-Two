About Me

My name is Saleema, I am 24 living in Toronto my whole life. I am a beginner in the VFX industry, and continuing my education; after graduating from Toronto Film School with a diploma in Visual Effects for TV & Film. I decided to further continue my education in this field while looking for work to help improve my skills as well as learn new ones. I never knew what I wanted to do, after changing careers 5 times, I finally realized the love I have for art, the overall effects and amount of work it takes to bring a scene or shot together within a film or episode.
